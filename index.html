LearnAbled

LearnAbled is an accessible online platform for Persons with Disabilities to upskill themselves. Hundreds of graduates have undertaken different courses like Python, Tally, BFSI, Digital Marketing etc. to make themselves industry ready. The purpose of LearnAbled is to increase the talent pool with disabilities and also to enhance the quality of applicants. The admission in LearnAbled courses is through rigorous eligibility criteria and/ or entrance examination.

These courses are of duration of 1 month to 6 months and equips students in today’s competitive world. All courses have soft skills module to further hone the personality of our students. Under this platform we also train candidates in skills as required by Corporate partner. As per the hiring needs we can train potential employees with disabilities in specific skill sets thereby increasing the probability of selection.
